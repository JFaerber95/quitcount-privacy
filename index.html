QuitCount — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

The Short Version

QuitCount does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Period.

Data Collection

QuitCount does not collect any personal information. All data you enter into the app (habits, streaks, spending amounts) is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

Analytics & Tracking

QuitCount does not use any analytics services, advertising frameworks, or tracking tools. There are no third-party SDKs in the app.

Data Storage

All app data is stored exclusively on your device. If you delete the app, your data is deleted with it. We have no servers, no databases, and no way to access your information.

Third-Party Services

QuitCount does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

Children's Privacy

QuitCount does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.
